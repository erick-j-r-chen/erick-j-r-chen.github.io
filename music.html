Erick has a passion for music in many forms. He is a multi-instrumentalist who plays the bass guitar and has learned the violin and piano. In high school, Erick played in the school orchestra, jazz ensemble, and Recyclable Percussion Club, where he served as Co-Vice President and helped create various instruments from recyclable items. Moreover, during his one year studying abroad in Tokyo, he joined a music-related club at his univeristy, playing at live houses around Tokyo from time to time. In his spare time, he likes to beatbox, and make avant-garde melodies on SoundCloud that are influenced by mainly jazz and world music.
