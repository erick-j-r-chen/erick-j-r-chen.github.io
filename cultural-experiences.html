Erick has a passion for international exchange, including cross-cultural research and travel. At USC, Erick serves as Co-President of the Exchange Student Supporters, a student club which aims to serve as a platform for international exchange students to interact with USC students. Other examples of Erick's cross-cultural experiences include interning at the Keio Academy of New York's 2018 Summer Program, where he contributed by helping to create a PR video, highlighting the bonds between the Japanese and American students. As further proof of his interest in international exchange, Erick also studied abroad in Tokyo during his junior year. In the future, he hopes to pursue a career where he can continue to participate in cross-cultural communication.
