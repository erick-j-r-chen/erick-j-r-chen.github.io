Erick Chen is a current senior at the University of Southern California in Los Angeles. He is an East Asian Area Studies major with minors in Digital Studies and TESOL. He was born in Santa Monica but grew up in Taipei, Taiwan. As such, he is interested in the crossroads between East and West. Some of his hobbies include playing the bass, traveling, cooking, and learning languages.


陳浚瑞是一位美國出生的台灣人. 他現在在洛杉磯的南加州大學讀東亞研究. 他的副專科是英文教育跟數位研究. 他沒事的時候喜歡彈電貝斯, 學語言, 旅行, 煮飯等等的. 他希望以後可以對台美關係有貢獻.
